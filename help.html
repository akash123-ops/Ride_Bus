Popular Help Articles

How do I change my booking details?

You can modify your booking details up to 6 hours before departure by logging into your account and accessing the "My Bookings" section. Some changes may incur modification fees depending on the operator's policy.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by operator. Generally, cancellations made 24+ hours before departure receive 80% refund, 6-24 hours before get 50% refund, and less than 6 hours are non-refundable. Check your ticket for specific terms.

How long does it take to receive a refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation approval. The time to appear in your account depends on your bank/payment method (up to additional 5-7 days for some banks).

Why can't I track my bus?

Tracking may be unavailable if: (1) The bus hasn't started its journey yet, (2) The GPS device is temporarily offline, or (3) You entered incorrect tracking details. Try again later or contact support.

How do I use a promo code?

During checkout, look for the "Apply Promo Code" field, enter your code, and click apply. The discount will be reflected in your total. Ensure the code is valid for your route and travel date.
